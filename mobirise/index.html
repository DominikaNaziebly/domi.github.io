island Series

Shapes and colours which make you feel calm, fulfilled, complete characterize my works.
My latest collection - Island series - is strongly connected with living on the island. I transform wild life into minimal shapes by natural colour palette.
I strongly believe that the painting process is a transfer of energy between painter and art consumer. The artwork is only the tool toward this experience.

about me

Currently I am based in Warsaw. Summer months I spend on the sailing yacht somewhere between greek islands.

I graduated the Academy of Fine Art in Łódź, Poland in 2010. Last ten years I dedicated to fashion , but this year came to me necessity to develop my art world through painting. Since June 2022 , I've been working as full time painter and have a great pleasure with it.
